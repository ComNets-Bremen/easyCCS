About the general idea and structure

  • A Skill describes kind of knowledge. Skills are names or identifiers and should describe the kind of knowledge.
  • A Content object contains material to teach certain skills. It can also require skills to successfully work on this content object.
  • A Module contains several content objects and is used to group related content objects. Like content objects, the modules also have required and new skills.
